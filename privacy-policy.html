Privacy Policy

eMAG Cleaner Chrome Extension

Last Updated: November 22, 2025

TL;DR: This extension does not collect, store, transmit, or share any user data whatsoever. Your privacy is completely protected.

1. Introduction

Thank you for using eMAG Cleaner ("the Extension"). This privacy policy explains how the Extension handles user data and privacy. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices.

2. Data Collection

The Extension does not collect any user data. Specifically:

  • We do not collect personal information (names, email addresses, phone numbers, etc.)
  • We do not collect browsing history or activity
  • We do not collect search queries or shopping behavior
  • We do not track your location
  • We do not collect device information
  • We do not use cookies or similar tracking technologies

3. How the Extension Works

eMAG Cleaner operates entirely locally on your device. The Extension:

  • Filters promoted products and third-party vendor items on eMAG.ro pages
  • Stores your filter preferences locally in your browser using Chrome's storage API
  • Only operates when you visit eMAG.ro websites
  • Does not communicate with any external servers
  • Does not transmit any data outside of your browser

4. Permissions Explained

The Extension requests the following permissions, which are used solely for its core functionality:

  • storage: To save your filter preferences locally on your device
  • activeTab: To access and filter content on eMAG.ro pages when you visit them

These permissions do not allow the Extension to access data from other websites or transmit any information externally.

5. Data Storage

The only data stored by the Extension is your personal preferences (such as which filters you have enabled). This data is:

  • Stored locally in your browser using Chrome's storage API
  • Never transmitted to any server or third party
  • Completely under your control and can be cleared at any time by uninstalling the Extension

6. Third-Party Services

The Extension does not integrate with, communicate with, or share data with any third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks.

7. Children's Privacy

The Extension does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. Since we collect no data at all, the Extension is safe for users of all ages.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

9. Your Rights

Since the Extension does not collect any user data, there is no personal data to access, modify, or delete. You maintain complete control over the Extension by:

  • Enabling or disabling the Extension at any time through Chrome's extension settings
  • Uninstalling the Extension to remove all locally stored preferences

This Extension complies with:

  • Chrome Web Store Developer Program Policies

Since no data is collected, there are no data protection concerns or compliance issues related to user privacy.
